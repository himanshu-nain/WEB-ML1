ABOUT CONFERENCE

Machine learning is an emerging area in research due to its application in diverse research areas. The machine learning techniques can be applied to various areas in computer science. The International Conference on Machine Learning and Data Analytics (ICMLDA 2019) will provide a wide platform to facilitate researchers from academia, industry and government organization to share their novel ideas. This conference aims to provide an international forum for contemplating the interdependent and inter-disciplinary research ideas for enhanced quality work on information processing. Thus, we welcome diverse innovative ideas towards the furtherance of this aim.

    PAPER SUBMISSION

    The paper will be submitted through Microsoft CMT which has its own similarity checker for plagiarism. The similarity score will be kept as per international norms (below 15%). The papers will be reviewed thoroughly. Each paper will be reviewed at least by 3 reviewers from the same area for making the review process fair.
